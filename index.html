GAMES





POKER

Use a standard western card set of 52 cards. At the beginning of each hand, each player will be given their own cards. And then on the table will flip out the common cards.the person with the strongest set of cards, which are combined from the individual hand on the hand and the common hand on the table is the winner.



BACCARAT

In Baccarat Scratch Card game, the Player or Banker door wins when the total number of cards divided by each door reaches a higher score and the highest score is 9 points. Tableau rules will not be changed and no decision will be made by the Player.



ROULETTE

In this game, players can choose to bet on one or more bet categories such as unique numbers, different groups of numbers, red or black, whether the number is odd or even, the number is high (19–36) or low (1 –18).
